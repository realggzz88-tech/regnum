About
E-COMMERCE ENTERPRISE

Unified Data & Marketing Automation

Infrastructure Challenge

Our client, a mid-market e-commerce enterprise, operated with customer data fragmented across 5 disconnected platforms: Shopify for transactions, Mailchimp for email marketing, Google Analytics for web behavior, Excel spreadsheets for manual reporting, and a legacy SQL database from their previous system. This created massive operational inefficiency�no single source of truth for customer data existed.

The marketing team had no visibility into customer purchase history when executing campaigns. The sales team couldn't see email engagement data when prioritizing outreach. Customer service lacked context on previous interactions. Campaign performance measurement was conducted manually through weekly spreadsheet aggregation, making real-time optimization impossible. Revenue growth stalled as the team spent more time managing data silos than executing strategy.

System Architecture Deployed

Centralized Customer Data Platform

We implemented Salesforce as the unified customer data hub, integrating all 5 existing platforms through custom API connections. Every customer touchpoint�website visits, email engagement, purchase transactions, support tickets�now syncs to a single customer record. This created the 360-degree customer view that was previously impossible with fragmented systems.

Automated Behavioral Segmentation

Built automated segmentation logic that classifies customers based on purchase frequency, average order value, product category preferences, and engagement patterns. Segments update in real-time as customer behavior changes. This replaced manual list building and enabled dynamic, behavior-triggered marketing campaigns that respond to actual customer actions.

Multi-Channel Marketing Automation

Deployed integrated marketing automation across email, SMS, and social channels�all orchestrated from Salesforce Marketing Cloud. Campaigns trigger automatically based on customer lifecycle stage, cart abandonment, product browsing behavior, and purchase history. Personalized content is dynamically inserted based on customer segment and previous interactions.

Real-Time Analytics Dashboard

Created custom reporting dashboards providing real-time visibility into campaign performance, customer lifetime value by segment, channel attribution, and revenue forecasting. Marketing and sales teams now make data-driven decisions based on live data instead of week-old spreadsheets. Executive leadership has real-time visibility into key growth metrics.
