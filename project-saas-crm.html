B2B SAAS PLATFORM

CRM Architecture Deployment

Infrastructure Challenge

A B2B SaaS company with a 12-person sales team operated without centralized CRM infrastructure. Deal tracking occurred across individual rep spreadsheets, email threads, and informal notes. No standardized pipeline stages existed. Follow-up protocols varied by rep preference. Revenue forecasting required manual aggregation from multiple sources.

The operational friction was quantifiable: approximately 8-10 hours per week spent on administrative tasks that should have been automated. Deal slippage occurred due to inconsistent follow-up. Sales leadership lacked real-time visibility into pipeline health. Onboarding new reps took 90+ days due to the absence of structured processes.

The company had reached $3M ARR but could not scale beyond current headcount without system infrastructure. The lack of operational clarity created a ceiling: hiring more reps meant more chaos, not more revenue.

System Architecture Deployed

CRM Infrastructure

Custom HubSpot CRM implementation configured to match the client's sales methodology. Five-stage pipeline architecture: Lead â†’ Qualified â†’ Demo Scheduled â†’ Proposal Sent â†’ Closed Won/Lost. Each stage included automated data validation to enforce quality standards. Deal records structured with mandatory fields to ensure complete information capture.

Automation Layer

Automated lead scoring based on engagement metrics (email opens, website visits, demo attendance) and firmographic data (company size, industry, budget tier). Workflow automation for deal stage progression with trigger-based task assignments. Email sequences integrated with CRM activity tracking to monitor rep engagement quality.

Integration Architecture

Bi-directional data synchronization between HubSpot CRM and the marketing automation platform (Marketo). Real-time lead handoff from marketing to sales with context preservation. Sales activity data flowing back to marketing for campaign optimization and lead source analysis.

Reporting & Dashboards

Real-time revenue dashboards for sales leadership displaying: pipeline value by stage, deal velocity metrics, individual rep performance, forecast accuracy tracking, and lead source ROI. Custom reports automated and delivered weekly to eliminate manual data aggregation.

Training & Adoption

Structured 2-week onboarding program for existing reps. Video documentation for all core workflows. Weekly office hours during the first 60 days to address adoption friction. Success metrics tracked: CRM login frequency, deal update consistency, and data quality scores.
